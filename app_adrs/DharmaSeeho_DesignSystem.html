Motion
Quick, snappy transitions feel responsive to children. Avoid slow, cinematic animations — they feel sluggish on mid-range Android phones.
TokenDurationEasingUsage
instant80msease-outButton press feedback, tap highlight
fast150msease-outCard press scale, pill select
default250msspring (tension:65, friction:11)Screen slide-in, modal appear
slow400msease-in-outProgress bar fill, XP counter
celebration600msspring bounceBadge earned, perfect score
pulse800ms loopease-in-outPlaying audio indicator, active state
Haptics: Use Haptics.impactAsync(Light) on slide advance. Haptics.notificationAsync(Success) on story completion or badge earn. Haptics.impactAsync(Medium) on correct quiz answer. No haptic on wrong answers — avoid punishing feeling.
Grid
Frame: 390px wide. 16px gutters left/right. Variable columns based on context.
1-column (default) — full-width cards, CTAs
Content width: 358px (390 − 16 − 16)
2-column — story grid, stat cards, game cards
Each column: 171px (358 − 8 gap) ÷ 2
3-column — badge grid, stat mini cards
Each column: 112px. Minimum tap target: 44px ✓
4-column — avatar row, dot pagination

Touch target rule: All tappable elements must be ≥44×44px. If a visual element is smaller (e.g. a dot), expand its hitbox with padding. This is non-negotiable for age 3–6 screens where children have imprecise motor control.
